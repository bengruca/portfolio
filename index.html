About

I'm a corporate communications professional who has spent the better part of a decade moving between some of the most interesting organizations in entertainment and media — from the hallways of 30 Rock during a global crisis, to the studio lot at Universal City, to A-list talent management in Beverly Hills.

My background bridges executive communications, brand partnerships, and strategic storytelling. I've managed multi-million dollar campaign budgets, produced pitch decks that closed deals, and written the kind of crisis communications that land on a CEO's desk at 6 a.m.

I'm fluent in both the creative and corporate sides of the entertainment business, and that's a rare combination.

Former Creative Artists Agency intern with existing relationships across the agency. Ready to hit the ground running.
